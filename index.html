C-Quest is one of the leading cost consultants in the Middle East, offering years of experience on some of the most ambitious, complex and schedule-accelerated projects in the world. We are gulf based and fully registered in each domain of our operations. We work with clients across Middle East on a wide range of private and public sector projects.

We offer unique cost and time saving solutions to a diverse range of needs and strive to provide accurate analysis of the current industry trends to ensure our clients interests are continually met. We focus on minimising risk and creating opportunities to maximise the value of our clients developments and property assets.

We have a set of organisational values that encompass everything we do redarding our clients, our staff, the quality of our work and sustainability. Our values were established through an organisation-wide staff consultation and a series of values workshops, which involved members of staff at all levels.
