C-Quest is an asset management and cost consultancy with fully operational offices across the MENA region. We work with clients across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia on a wide range of private and public sector projects. We have a well-earned reputation for the quality of our cost management, value engineering and procurement strategy advisory services.

We offer unique cost and time saving solutions to a diverse range of needs and strive to provide accurate analysis of the current industry trends to ensure our clients interests are continually met. We focus on minimising risk and creating opportunities to maximise the value of our Clients developments and property assets.
